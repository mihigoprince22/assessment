About Us

Mihigo Prince

Project Manager & Real Estate Mogul

After finishing his studies in the field of Project Management at Kepler College, Mr. Mihigo started his successful career and started a few businesses. His first position was a BAG Ambassador for a period of 6 months. He later moved on to MTN as an intern and gained a good position in the human resource department. He then managed to move to Germany to start his career in sports management. Where he helped to restructure Hamburg Football Club, and brought different innovative projects that helped the club to be promoted into the Bundesliga once again. He also managed to start up a 3D printing business in the Medical field which is called Rwanda Med 3D Limited, which is one of the most successful companies in Africa. After selling his shares in the company he started investing in the housing and real estate sector where he now owns a building in every country
